### Trading Journal & Verdict Engine Improvements

#### 1. Sticky Verdict Engine

* Make the **Verdict Engine remain visible while scrolling**.
* It should be fixed or sticky so I can continuously monitor the trade score, confidence percentage, and verdict without needing to scroll back up.
* The Verdict Engine should not obstruct important content but should always be accessible.

#### 2. Replace Analytics Center with Trading Journal

* Remove the current **Analytics Center** section entirely.
* Replace it with a **Trading Journal** feature.
* The Trading Journal should automatically compile all trade information entered into the application, including:

  * Market bias per timeframe
  * Checklist results
  * Entry details
  * Risk management details
  * Final verdict
  * Notes and observations

#### 3. Downloadable Trade Journal

* Add a **"Download Journal"** button.
* When clicked, generate a professional trade journal that can be exported as:

  * PDF (preferred)
  * Image format (optional)
* The journal should be formatted cleanly and be suitable for record-keeping and performance reviews.

#### 4. Screenshot Support per Timeframe

* Allow image uploads for each timeframe analysis (4H, 1H, 15M, etc.).
* These screenshots should be:

  * Displayed within the application
  * Automatically included in the exported PDF journal
* The final PDF should show each screenshot alongside its corresponding analysis and notes.

#### 5. Remove Position Size Calculator

* Remove the Position Size Calculator section completely.
* The trading checklist and trade validation process are sufficient for my workflow.

#### 6. Mobile Optimization

* On mobile devices, the Verdict Engine should not appear as a normal section that requires additional scrolling.
* Instead, display it as a floating overlay positioned at the bottom-right corner of the screen.
* The overlay should:

  * Remain visible while scrolling
  * Show the current confidence score and verdict
  * Be compact and non-intrusive
  * Expand when tapped to show full details

#### 7. User Experience Goal

The application should function primarily as a **trade execution and journaling tool**, allowing me to:

1. Analyze a setup.
2. Complete the checklist.
3. Receive a verdict score.
4. Attach timeframe screenshots.
5. Export everything into a professional trading journal PDF for future review.

The focus should be on documentation, review, and decision-making rather than analytics dashboards.
